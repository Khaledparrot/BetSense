Problem Gambling Self-Assessment

Answer these questions honestly to assess if you might have a gambling problem:

1. Have you ever felt the need to bet more and more money?

2. Have you ever lied to cover up the amount of time or money you spent gambling?

3. Have you ever borrowed money to gamble or pay gambling debts?

4. Has gambling ever caused you to neglect work, school or family responsibilities?

5. Have you ever felt guilty about the way you gamble or what happens when you gamble?
