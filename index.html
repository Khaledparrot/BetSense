Responsible Gambling Principles

Time Management

Set time limits for your gambling sessions and stick to them to maintain balance in your life.

Budget Control

Only gamble with money you can afford to lose, and never chase losses with more gambling.

Risk Awareness

Understand that gambling is entertainment, not a way to make money, and the odds always favor the house.

Self-Care

Recognize warning signs of problem gambling and seek help early if gambling stops being fun.
