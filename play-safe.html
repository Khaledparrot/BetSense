Responsible Gambling Guide

Learn how to enjoy gambling safely with our comprehensive responsible gambling strategies.

Essential Safety Tips

1. Set Limits Before You Play

Decide on time and money limits before you start gambling. Stick to these limits no matter what happens during your session.

2. Never Chase Losses

Accept that losing is part of gambling. Trying to win back losses often leads to bigger losses and problematic behavior.

3. Balance Gambling with Other Activities

Make sure gambling doesn't replace hobbies, social activities, or work responsibilities in your life.

4. Understand the Games

Know the rules and odds of games you play. The house always has an edge - gambling should be entertainment, not income.

Warning Signs of Problem Gambling

Behavioral Signs

  • • Gambling with increasing amounts of money
  • • Failed attempts to control or stop gambling
  • • Restlessness or irritability when trying to stop

Financial Signs

  • • Borrowing money to gamble or pay debts
  • • Selling possessions to get gambling money
  • • Hiding gambling losses from family

Emotional Signs

  • • Gambling to escape problems or relieve stress
  • • Feeling guilty or depressed after gambling
  • • Lying about gambling activities

Social Signs

  • • Neglecting work or family for gambling
  • • Relationship problems due to gambling
  • • Isolation from non-gambling friends
